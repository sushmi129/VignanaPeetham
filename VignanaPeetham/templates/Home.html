VIGNANA PEETHAM 

         ​Vignana Peetham, a Vishwa Hindu Parishad-directed Children's Care Home, Aided Primary School & High School is managed by G.P.R. Charities Trust which was instituted by Sri G.Pulla Reddy in 1974 with the motto of rendering service to society. Sri G.Pulla Reddy is the epitome of a self-made man, a renowned philanthropist and a humanist. His love for education and care for his native district manifested themselves in the form of this society. Born into a lower-middle-class agricultural family, he imbibed all the virtues of an Indian farmer and subsequently turned into one of the important pillars of the society, especially in the field of social welfare. The primary objective of the Vignana Peetham is to encourage, foster, and promote all activities for the upliftment of the orphan children with a view to integrating them with the rest of the society and bringing them into the mainstream of national life and other activities to promote the well-being and interests of the society.
