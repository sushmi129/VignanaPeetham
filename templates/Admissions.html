Admissions into the orphanage

Our Home has been working in collaboration with key stakeholders to drive a clear value proposition and deeper impact for the underprivileged section of the society through innovative program design, robust monitoring processes, upholding utmost transparency and accountability in its operations with long term, strategic and sustainable approach to inclusive development


  Eligibility :
  • Only boys belonging to poor families of the following categories are admitted into the Children Care Home and provided free meals, accommodation, clothing and education from Class 1 to Class 10.
  • Boys who have lost both parents.
    •
  • Boys who have lost father/mother, preference being given to the boys who have lost their father.
  • Boys whose parents are incapacitated due to chronic diseases or physical disabilities.
    •
  • The decision to approve the admission is done by a selection committee.
    •
         Admissions are usually made in June, at the beginning of the academic year, so that the boys admitted in the children care home could be admitted in the schools. (Rules may vary due to covid-19 pandemic)
